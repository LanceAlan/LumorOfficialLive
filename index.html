Lumor

Lumor is building PRE — a new physical substrate for computation designed to move beyond brittle digital stacks and into adaptive, energy-efficient, real-world architectures.

Who We Are

Lumor is an early-stage research company exploring new physical approaches to computation. Our work focuses on foundational architectures rather than incremental improvements.

What We Are Building

PRE is our ongoing investigation into a physical computational medium designed for adaptive, resilient, real-world systems. The specifics will evolve as the research develops.

What We Aim to Achieve

Our long-term goal is to create computational substrates that operate beyond the limits of traditional digital stacks — systems that can adapt, stabilize, and interact with the world in fundamentally new ways.

Founder

Lumor was founded by Lance, a systems architect focused on building environments, tools, and computational frameworks that enable new forms of intelligence and interaction. A full bio will be added as the project matures.
